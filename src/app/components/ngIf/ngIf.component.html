----------------------- What is Structural Directive -----------------------



In Angular, structural directives are used to change the structure of the DOM by adding or removing elements based on certain conditions. They control the layout or the presence of elements in the DOM. They essentially manipulate the structure of the DOM tree.

Key Points about Structural Directives:
1. They add, remove, or manipulate elements in the DOM. They are prefixed with an asterisk (*).
2. They typically work by adding or removing elements based on conditions or loops.
